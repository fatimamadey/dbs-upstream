BREAKING: Major Cloud Provider Reports Global Outage Affecting Millions

LIVE COVERAGE
By Sarah Chen, Infrastructure Desk LAST UPDATED: 2 MIN AGO

A major cloud services provider is experiencing a widespread global outage that has knocked offline millions of websites, enterprise applications, and consumer services. The incident, which began at approximately 9:14 AM EDT, is being described as one of the most significant infrastructure failures in recent memory.

Engineering teams have identified the root cause as a cascading failure in the provider's US-East region that propagated to backup systems in Europe and Asia-Pacific. Multiple Fortune 500 companies have reported complete service disruptions. Financial markets are reacting sharply, with the provider's stock down 7.3% in midday trading. The company's status page currently lists 23 of 28 core services as experiencing "major outage" conditions. A spokesperson said a full postmortem would be published within 72 hours of resolution.
